Our Discipleship Studies

We dig deep into the Word of God - improving our inductive study approach with each passage within context while breaking down each verse to gain a greater understanding of His teachings.

Discipleship is simply a development program. Technically speaking, it is an apprenticeship to master the disciplines under our Savior Jesus Christ. When we enter into discipleship with an apostle, the goal is to be more like Jesus Christ, the ultimate teacher. Like any development program, there must be structure. Here is our training schedule throughout the school year for new Impact students. Notice that we start at the basics no matter what our spiritual maturity level is because of the need for clear understanding across ministries. These basics evolve from our core values. Because we apply each topic learned through our on-hands training, we call each lesson a "practice". And if you're not practicing to win, then you're unconsciously preparing to lose.

#2Biblical Study Methods To practice studying the Bible daily1 on his/her own.
1 The aim is to actually study almost everyday and to have our minds dwelling on the Word during our days where we are either resting from studying or have ran out of time in the day to study (considering many students of the Word are quite busy executing their calling from God).
